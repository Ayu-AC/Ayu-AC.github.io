About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the National University of Defense Technology (NUDT), supervised by Prof. Ji Wang. I am also a one-year visiting Ph.D. student at Singapore Management University (SMU), co-supervised by Prof. Jun Sun, sponsored by the China Scholarship Council (CSC) Joint PhD Program. Previously, I earned my B.Sc. in Software Engineering from Hunan University (HNU).

Research Interests

My research aims to provide formal trustworthiness guarantees for machine learning components, particularly within complex learning-enabled software systems.

Precondition Synthesis & Contract Verification
Formally verifying and synthesizing preconditions for deep neural networks.
Runtime Monitoring & Shielding
Developing robust runtime mechanisms to ensure the safe execution and control of learning-enabled systems.
Trustworthy AI Formal Methods Software Engineering for AI Neuro-Symbolic Programming
I am always open to collaborations. Please feel free to reach out via email or schedule an online meeting 😃!
